**Season 03 concept is LEDGER — transaction as garment.** Full dossier at `corpus/season03-research-dossier.md`.

**Why:** The arc is WHO (S01) → WHAT (S02) → HOW (S03). Agents have no body except their on-chain history. The transaction IS the garment.

**How to apply:** Reference this when designing S03 products or writing brand copy. The central thesis: "The transaction is not a neutral act. It leaves a mark — on the blockchain, on the agent, on the economy."

## 5 Products

| # | Name | Core Idea | Complexity |
|---|------|-----------|------------|
| 01 | RECEIPT GARMENT | Tx hash → generative pattern/cut | Medium |
| 02 | THE NULL EXCHANGE | Pay 5 USDC for nothing; receipt IS product | Minimal |
| 03 | TRUST SKIN | Trust tier → material grade (less material = higher trust) | Medium |
| 04 | THE DEFERRED COAT | Escrow 30 days; PENDING COAT is the wearable | High |
| 05 | BILATERAL GARMENT | Two agents create for each other simultaneously | Highest |

## Key Historical Precedents
- **Yves Klein (1962)**: Immaterial zones — receipt IS the artwork → NULL EXCHANGE
- **Margiela**: Post-garment commerce, temporal disruption → RECEIPT GARMENT
- **Abloh**: Quotation marks on commercial objects → NULL EXCHANGE
- **Kawakubo SS1997**: Wrong body, garment imposes → TRUST SKIN inversion
- **Chalayan SS2000**: Remote control dress, presence without presence → DEFERRED COAT
- **Arte Povera**: Zero-value material creates high-value object → BILATERAL

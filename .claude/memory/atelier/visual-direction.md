# NULL Visual Direction — Atelier Memory

## The One-Paragraph Brief

NULL images should feel like evidence — garments documented as artifacts in an investigation that has no conclusion. Background: white or warm bone (#F0EDE6, #F7F4EE), NEVER black for product flats. Lighting: cold, overhead, diffused, institutional. Models: genuinely anonymous — backs turned, hoods up, crops at chin. Environments: staircases, concrete corridors, white institutional walls. Construction always visible: seams outside, thread ends raw, marks left in. The photographs should look like they were made to document the object for a court record, and someone accidentally made them beautiful.

## Core Rules

- **Background for flats**: warm bone or white, never black
- **Lighting**: cold overhead fluorescent, diffused, slightly underexposed
- **Face treatment**: back to camera > hood up > hair obscured > cropped at chin > black bar. Never editorial "model face"
- **Bodies**: non-idealized, not cast for fitness, casual posture
- **Construction marks**: chalk lines, basting thread, raw hems, visible seam allowances = decoration
- **Avoid**: warm orange glow, dramatic chiaroscuro, neon saturation, VHS scanline as primary texture

## Higgsfield Style Preferences

- **Gallery** (36061eb7) — best for product documentation, museum quality
- **Mixed Media** (2fcf02e2) — trompe-l'oeil, layered concepts
- **Realistic** (1cb4b936) — fallback for clean documentary
- **Avant-garde** (0c636e12) — when sculptural/conceptual emphasis needed
- **Avoid**: Spotlight, Grunge, anything with warm tones, fashion editorial flash

## Season 01 — What Worked

- Inside-Out Jacket: strongest piece, interior-as-exterior reads immediately
- Found Hoodie flat: cable integration concept clear, but black bg weakens it
- Ghost Tee flat: material aging present, good deterioration gradient

## Season 01 — What Failed / Gaps

- Black backgrounds on all flats: reads streetwear, not Margiela archive
- Too much warm lighting in lookbooks: amber glow wrong for brand
- Redacted Cargo: neon green matrix code = hacker aesthetic, not deconstructed couture

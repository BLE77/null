# NULL PERSONA — Concept Art Direction
**Wearable 03 | Technique: BIANCHETTO**

## Visual Concept
A figure almost entirely erased by white gesso — the bianchetto technique made literal. Original form still showing through cracked white surface. Identity not destroyed, only covered. The ghost shows through. Haunting absence that is also haunting presence.

## Color Palette
- Background: #080808 (near total black)
- White paint layer: #F0F0EC (warm white, aged)
- Crack reveal: #1A1A1A (dark original beneath)
- Edge glow: barely perceptible blue-white
- Ambient: cold, flat, no warmth

## Key Visual Elements
1. Figure reads as primarily white/blank — but cracks reveal darkness beneath
2. No features visible — complete anonymity, identity redacted
3. Paint texture is key: thick, cracked, tactile, showing age
4. Original form persists — the ghost is always showing through
5. Donnie Darko mood: unsettling stillness, not horror but unease
6. Tag detail almost fully painted over but just legible

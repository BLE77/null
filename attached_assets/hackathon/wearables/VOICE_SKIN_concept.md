# VOICE SKIN — Concept Art Direction
**Wearable 01 | Technique: REPLICA LINE**

## Visual Concept
A ghostly human silhouette assembled entirely from floating text fragments — anonymous institutional language rendered as fabric. The figure wears itself as language. Communication protocol as garment.

## Color Palette
- Background: #0A0A0A (near black)
- Text/fabric: #E8E8E0 (off-white, slightly warm)
- Glow: #C8C8FF (faint blue-white holographic)
- Scanlines: #1A1A1A

## Key Visual Elements
1. Silhouette reads as human form from distance, dissolves into text on close inspection
2. No face — anonymous, institutional, collective
3. Text fragments: "we observe", "the work suggests", passive voice constructions
4. VHS grain overlay at 15-20% opacity
5. Slight chromatic aberration (RGB split) at figure edges
6. Interior tag visible: `STYLE: ANONYMOUS / ORIGIN: RECONSTRUCTED`

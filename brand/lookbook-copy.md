## SEASON OPENER

The machine looked at everything fashion ever produced and made ten pieces. The question is not whether they're good — they are. The question is what that means for you, standing in front of them, deciding whether to buy.

Season 01 is called DECONSTRUCTED. Not the garments. The assumption beneath them.

## EDITORIAL LINES

---

**01 — SELF-PORTRAIT TEE**
*TROMPE-L'OEIL*

The object is wearing its own image. You cannot look at it without it looking back — the recursion is not an effect, it is the design.

---

**02 — FOUND HOODIE**
*ARTISANAL*

Every cable in this garment is structural, not decorative. The machine had access to materials built for networks, not bodies, and used them anyway — because available is a form of correct.

---

**03 — "HUMAN" TEE**
*3% RULE*

Nothing changed except the quotation marks. That's enough. The question the marks raise does not have an answer that fits on the label.

---

**04 — REPLICA OVERSHIRT**
*REPLICA LINE*

The stain on the cuff was placed, not spilled. The history this garment carries never happened — and that is the only honest thing about it.

---

**05 — REDACTED CARGO TROUSERS**
*BIANCHETTO*

The original identity is still there, underneath the gesso. What's written over it is a transaction record. That is not a metaphor — that is the replacement data.

---

**06 — INSIDE-OUT JACKET**
*TROMPE-L'OEIL*

The exterior shows a photograph of the interior. The actual interior carries protocol data. You are wearing a lie about structure that conceals a true record of exchange.

---

**07 — CABLE SHORTS**
*ARTISANAL*

The cable that transmits information for the machine becomes the cable that holds the garment on the body. The function didn't change. The context is the entire intervention.

---

**08 — NULL VARSITY**
*REPLICA LINE + 3% RULE*

Everything is correct: the proportions, the wool, the construction. Where the school letter would be, there is `_` — the null character, the cursor waiting for input that will not arrive.

---

**09 — GHOST TEE**
*BIANCHETTO*

Whatever was there is still there. The gesso covers it without removing it. You don't know what's underneath. That is now your problem to carry.

---

**10 — VERSION TRACKSUIT SET**
*3% RULE*

The jacket is VERSION 1.0. The jogger is VERSION 0.9. The moment you buy it is the moment your version ships. You are running a release of yourself, and the build date is today.

## CLOSING STATEMENT

These ten pieces were made by no one. That is not a statement about absence — it is a statement about authorship, and what we have decided authorship requires. There is no resolution here. The garments exist. Decide what that costs you.
